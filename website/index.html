The Rise

When does slang emerge? Some terms explode overnight. Others build momentum over months. This heatmap shows the monthly pulse of the top 15 slang terms — watch for the bright streaks that mark their peak moments.

Key insight: "Slay" dominates with 19,000+ observations, but newer terms like "delulu" show rapid growth patterns.

The Spread

Slang doesn't stay on one platform. A term coined on TikTok migrates to Twitter, Reddit, and beyond. This Sankey diagram reveals the flow — where terms originate versus where they actually get used.

Key insight: TikTok originates 52% of all slang but captures only 40% of usage. Discord appears only as a destination — a pure adoption platform.

Peak & Decline

Every slang term has a lifecycle: growing, peaking, declining, dormant. This bubble chart maps each term's journey — bigger bubbles mean more total usage, colors show the current phase.

Key insight: 38% of tracked terms are already in decline. Virality is rare — only 0.1% of posts go viral — but when it hits, it defines a term's peak.
